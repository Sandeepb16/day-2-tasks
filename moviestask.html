Magadheera

Magadheera is an Indian Telugu film. Directed by S.S. Rajamouli and produced by Allu Arvind.
It is a historical love story. Starring Ram Charan Teja and Kajal Aggarwal in the lead roles .
The film was released in theaters on 31 July 2009.
