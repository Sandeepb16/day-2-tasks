Chickpet Donne Biryani House Restaurant

"The restaurant takes its name from the traditional 'donne' style of serving biryani,
 which involves serving the aromatic rice dish in a banana leaf cone, adding a unique and
 authentic touch to the dining experience. The ambiance of the restaurant is warm and inviting.
 , with a blend of rustic and modern decor elements. Accompanied by friendly staff and impeccable
 service, Chickpet Donne Biryani Restaurant is a must-visit for biryani enthusiasts.."
